Privacy Policy

Commission Tracker · last updated 2026

Commission Tracker (“the app”) helps a user track their own wireless-retail commission. This policy explains what the app stores and how it is used. We keep data collection to the minimum needed for the app to work.

What the app stores

  • On your device: the commission periods you save, your payout-rate settings, your monthly goal, and the barcodes the scanner has learned. This is kept locally in your browser and never leaves the device unless you turn on an account or an export.
  • In your account (optional): if you sign in, the same data above is also saved to your private cloud record so it syncs across your devices. Your sign-in email is stored to identify your account.

What we do not collect

  • No customer or sales-recipient personal information.
  • No location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising.
  • No selling or sharing of your data with third parties for marketing.

Accounts & access

Sign-in and cloud sync are handled by Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore). Your email and app data are stored in the app owner’s Firebase project and protected by security rules so only you — and an app administrator — can access your record. Access to the app is limited to an approved-users list maintained by the administrator.

Barcode lookups

When you scan a new barcode, the number may be sent to a third-party product database (UPCitemdb) to fetch the product name. Only the barcode number is sent; no personal data is included. If the lookup is unavailable, you can enter the name manually.

Camera

The barcode scanner uses your device camera only while the scan screen is open. Camera images are processed on-device to read the barcode and are not stored or transmitted.

Your choices

  • Use the app fully offline without an account — all data stays on your device.
  • Export a backup of your data at any time from Settings.
  • Ask the administrator to remove your account from the access list; deleting your account record removes your synced data.

Data retention

Local data remains until you clear it or uninstall the app. Account data remains until it is deleted by you or the administrator.

Contact
